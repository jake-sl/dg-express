About DG Express Transports

Based in Richmond, TX, DG Express Transports provides FMCSA-compliant hazmat trucking and box-truck freight services to shippers nationwide. Our fleet is built for hazardous materials, including flammable liquids, corrosives, and compressed gases. Every shipment moves under documented safety protocols, proper placarding, and carrier-grade insurance.

Nationwide coverage, Houston-based dispatch

We run freight across all 48 contiguous states, with centralized dispatch out of the Houston metro. Located in the heart of the Gulf Coast energy and chemical corridor.

DOT-Compliant Fleet

Purpose-equipped hazmat trucks and box trucks maintained to federal standards, with current inspections and proper placarding on every unit.

Nationwide Freight Coverage

Full truckload (FTL) hazmat and general freight service across all 48 contiguous states, dispatched from the Houston metro.

Endorsed, Trained Drivers

Every driver holds a CDL-A with active H & N endorsements, TSA Threat Assessment clearance, and FMCSA-required hazmat safety and security training.

Regulatory Compliance

We operate under valid USDOT and MC authority with a written hazmat security plan per 49 CFR 172 Subpart I, proper shipping papers, and emergency response documentation on every load.
