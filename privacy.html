Privacy Policy

This policy explains how DG Express Transports LLC collects, uses, and protects information on this site.

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit via the Contact form (name, message) and Quote form (name, email, phone, shipment details).
  • Application information you provide when applying for a role.
  • Consent choices for cookies, map embeds, and optional translation.

How we use it

  • Responding to inquiries, quotes, or applications.
  • Coordinating transportation services.
  • Improving site experience and honoring your consent settings.

Third-party services

  • Google Maps (only after you enable it) to display our location.
  • Optional website translator (if enabled by you).
  • Optional analytics/ads tools, only if you opt in.

These services may set cookies or collect usage data according to their own policies.

Consent and revocation

You can enable/disable Maps, translator, and analytics on the Cookie Preferences page at any time. Forms include explicit consent checkboxes where required. To withdraw consent or request deletion, contact us at support@dgexpresstransports.com.

Data retention & security

We retain inquiry and application details only as long as needed to respond or meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information but cannot guarantee absolute security over the internet.
